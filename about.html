Platform role

What Paneli helps with — and what it does not do.

This distinction keeps the website accurate, aggregator-safe, and useful for homeowners comparing siding provider options.

What Paneli helps with

Organized provider comparison.

Paneli helps homeowners compare independent siding provider options by service category, provider fit, and project questions.

  • Organize siding service categories.
  • Help homeowners start provider comparison.
  • Encourage direct verification.
  • Keep materials inside the service decision flow.
What Paneli does not do

No direct siding work.

Paneli is not a direct siding company and does not control provider estimates, schedules, warranties, or project execution.

  • Does not install siding directly.
  • Does not replace or repair siding directly.
  • Does not inspect, paint, maintain, or consult directly.
  • Does not guarantee provider work, pricing, or availability.
