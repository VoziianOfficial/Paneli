Privacy practices for a siding provider-matching website.

This Privacy Policy describes how Paneli may collect, use, and share information when users visit the website, submit a request, call, email, or interact with platform features.

1. Information we may collect

The website may collect information that users choose to provide through forms, phone calls, email links, or other contact methods.

  • Name, phone number, email address, and selected siding service category.
  • Message details related to siding installation, replacement, repair, or consultation comparison.
  • Basic browser, device, and usage information such as page visits, approximate interaction data, and preference choices.
  • Policy banner choices stored in local browser storage.
No direct siding service relationship

Submitting information through the website does not hire a siding contractor and does not create a direct service agreement with Paneli.

2. How information may be used

Information may be used to operate the website, respond to user requests, support provider matching, improve site experience, and maintain legal or security records.

  • To help organize siding provider comparison requests.
  • To respond to contact form submissions, calls, or emails.
  • To understand which service category a homeowner is interested in.
  • To improve website functionality, usability, and content clarity.
  • To maintain records needed for compliance, security, or dispute prevention.

3. How information may be shared

Information may be shared with independent providers, service vendors, or platform support partners when needed to operate the provider-matching experience.

  • Independent local siding-related companies that may respond to homeowner requests.
  • Technology, hosting, analytics, communications, or security vendors supporting the website.
  • Legal, compliance, or safety parties if required by applicable law or to protect platform rights.

Providers are independent companies. Paneli does not control provider pricing, estimates, schedules, warranties, communications, or work.

4. Cookies and local storage

The website may use cookies, browser storage, or similar technologies for basic preferences, policy banner choices, usability, and site performance.

For more details, review the Cookie Policy.

5. Data security

Reasonable safeguards may be used to help protect information. However, no website, storage system, or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Users should avoid submitting sensitive information that is not needed for siding provider comparison.

6. Data retention

Information may be retained for as long as reasonably needed to operate the website, respond to requests, maintain records, support compliance, resolve disputes, or improve platform functionality.

7. User choices

Users may choose not to submit a form. Users may also contact Paneli using the listed email address to request information about privacy-related questions.

  • Users can decline optional policy banner choices where available.
  • Users can disable cookies through browser settings.
  • Users can contact the platform by email for privacy-related requests.

8. Children’s privacy

This website is intended for adults seeking siding provider comparison options. It is not intended for children, and users should not submit information on behalf of minors unless they are legally authorized to do so.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates may be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact Paneli at hello@paneli.example.
